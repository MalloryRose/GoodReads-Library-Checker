Suggest that we buy it for the collection.

Alachua County residents with a library card in good standing and a $0 balance can request Interlibrary Loans (ILLs) from other library systems.
Search now for an Interlibrary Loan.

ILLs may include:
Physical books
Microfilm
Photocopies of articles or book chapters
No eBooks
No books published in last 6 months
No complete issues of magazines
No DVDs or CDs
No dissertations or master theses

You can call 352-334-3938 and leave a message, or email illref@aclib.us for more information. Alachua County Library District does not charge for ILL service, but other libraries might charge processing fees or fines on overdue ILLs. Overdue ILLs will immediately block your account. Renewals must be requested at least 3 business days before the due date, and ILLs should be returned at a check-in desk. Total ILL requests cannot exceed 10.
